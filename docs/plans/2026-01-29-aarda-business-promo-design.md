# Aarda for Business - Promotional Video Design

## Overview

**Duration:** 45 seconds (1350 frames @ 30fps)
**Formats:**
- Vertical 9:16 (1080x1920) - mobile/social
- Landscape 16:9 (1920x1080) - presentations/web
**Audience:** General B2B - Marketing, HR, Corporate Communications
**Style:** Flat illustration, warm corporate palette

## Visual Style

### Color Palette
- **Backgrounds:** Cream (#FDF6E9), Beige (#F5E6D3)
- **Warm accents:** Yellow (#FBBF24), Coral (#F97316), Terracotta (#EA580C)
- **Figures:** Burgundy (#7C2D12), Navy (#1E3A5A)
- **Pain scenes:** Desaturated greys (#9CA3AF, #6B7280, #4B5563)
- **Success accents:** Green (#22C55E)

### Illustration Style
- Flat, geometric figures (inspired by reference image)
- Simple shapes, no complex details
- Warm, approachable, professional
- Minimal gradients, clean edges

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Pain (0-20s, frames 0-600)
Heavy emphasis on frustration with static internal content.

**1.1 The Document Graveyard (0-7s, frames 0-210)**
- Office desk with laptop
- Documents flying in: "Compliance Update", "Policy v3.2", "ESG Report", "Company History"
- Stack grows, dust settles
- Figure with "TL;DR" thought bubble
- Caption: "Important content. Ignored by everyone."

**1.2 The Scroll of Death (7-13s, frames 210-390)**
- Phone/tablet showing endless PDF
- Tiny figure scrolling through grey text walls
- Progress bar barely moving
- Eyes glazing over
- Caption: "Too long. Too boring. Too forgettable."

**1.3 The Innovation Gap (13-20s, frames 390-600)**
- Split scene: competitors zooming forward (bright, speed lines)
- Your company stuck with static documents (grey)
- Clock ticking, world moving fast
- Headlines: "Digital Transformation", "Employee Engagement Crisis"
- Caption: "The world moves fast. Static content doesn't."

### Scene 2: The Shift (20-28s, frames 600-840)
Transformation moment - grey to full color.

**2.1 The Spark (20-23s, frames 600-690)**
- Lightbulb appears above figure
- Color bleeds outward from lightbulb
- Documents start transforming
- Caption: "What if your content came alive?"

**2.2 The Transformation (23-28s, frames 690-840)**
- PDF morphs into interactive world
- Portal opens from document
- Character steps INTO content
- Interactive elements: choices, conversations, branches
- Metrics going UP
- Caption: "Interactive. Memorable. Engaging."
